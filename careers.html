Working Together, Connecting &
Creating Safer Working Environments

We are focused on setting the standards for others to follow, and paramount to this are our teams of innovative, multiskilled, experienced and ambitious individuals. We value each member of the team and at CEG you are not just a number. Be a part of us. Be successful. Be CEG.

Reward + Recogination

It’s not just CEG as a company that is recognised for its achievements, individuals are too. Hard work doesn’t go unnoticed at CEG, we recognise and reward such individuals as well as providing opportunities for all with work benefits such training, education and more.

Innovation

Whether it’s for our teams or our clients we pioneer and see value in innovation that makes work systems easier, safer and more efficient.

Skills + Training

We believe in investing in the future and part of this is giving all the opportunity to train and learn, strengthening skills and experience in order to make our teams better. Currently we run graduate schemes, work placements and apprenticeships. Keep an eye on this Careers page or our LinkedIn page for more information and updates. When you succeed, we succeed.

Diversity + Women in Construction

We want to shout out that CEG, and the construction industry, is a great place to work for all individuals regardless of colour, race, religion, gender, age, nationality, sexual orientation, marital status or disability, whatever career path you decide to follow. Our teams work to uphold diversity and inclusion, and support equal opportunities. Roles are so varied across the CEG sectors as well as the actual projects themselves.

CEG have proudly been given the opportunity to work on some prestigious projects which many of our female members have played a strong part in delivering. We strive for continuing improvements in supporting women getting into construction and those seeking more senior roles. We are proud to showcase all individuals and teams at CEG. We are an equal opportunities employer.
