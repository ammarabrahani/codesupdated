LCEG is committed to high standards of corporate governance believing in integrity, transparency and ensuring legal compliance. We are a responsible business and ensure code of conduct best practice is agreed to and implemented by all members of our teams.

Health, Safety + Enivironment

CEG are committed to health, safety and environment through our Buildsmart BuildSafe strategy. Our award-winning strategy has driven safety initiatives into project sites making them safer and more efficient places to be for our staff and clients. We ensure all operatives are fully trained. Health, safety, environment and quality policies are in place and adhered to no matter what and our accreditations and associative memberships are indicative of our continued dedication to safety.
